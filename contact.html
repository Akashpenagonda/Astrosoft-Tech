Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common inquiries

What services does AstrosoftTech offer?

AstrosoftTech provides a comprehensive range of software development services including custom software development, mobile app development (iOS & Android), web development, AI & ML solutions, UI/UX design, cloud computing services, and IoT development. We tailor our services to meet your specific business requirements.

How can I get a quote for my project?

You can request a quote by filling out our contact form with details about your project, or you can email us directly at sales@astrosofttech.com. For more accurate estimates, we recommend scheduling a consultation call where we can discuss your requirements in detail.

What is your typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary depending on complexity, scope, and specific requirements. Small projects may take 4-8 weeks, medium projects 3-6 months, and large enterprise solutions 6-12 months or more. After our initial consultation, we'll provide you with a detailed project timeline.

Do you offer ongoing support after project completion?

Yes, we offer various support and maintenance packages tailored to your needs. These can include bug fixes, performance optimization, feature additions, security updates, and 24/7 technical support. We'll discuss these options with you before project completion.

What industries do you specialize in?

While we serve clients across various industries, we have particular expertise in FinTech, Healthcare, E-commerce, Education, and Enterprise SaaS solutions. Our team stays updated with industry-specific regulations and best practices to deliver compliant and effective solutions.
